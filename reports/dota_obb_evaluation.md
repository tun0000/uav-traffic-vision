Generated by `scripts/evaluate_dota_obb.py`. Uses ultralytics' built-in rotated-IoU OBB metrics via `model.val()` directly -- no separate pycocotools pipeline needed here, unlike the VisDrone axis-aligned-box track.

Reported here as the accepted, reproducible result rather than a still-improving intermediate checkpoint.
